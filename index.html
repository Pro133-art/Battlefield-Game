Battlefield Command

Deploy your line from the command deck.

Controls

  • Use the command deck across the top to deploy units.
  • Click a blue unit to select it.
  • Click the ground to move, or click an enemy to attack.
  • Hold W, A, S, D or move the mouse to the edge of the battlefield to pan.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom the camera.
  • Press 1 to spawn an infantry unit.
  • Press 2 to spawn a ranger unit.
  • Press Space to send the selected unit to the enemy base.
